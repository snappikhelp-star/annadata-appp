Add Supplier / Company Payment Management in Admin Panel.

Purpose:
Keshav bhai should easily track how much money he has to pay to seed, pesticide and fertilizer companies/suppliers.

Add new admin page:
“Company Payments”
Hindi: “Company ko Dena Hai”

Features:
1. Supplier/Company Management
- Add company/supplier name
- Mobile number
- Address
- Contact person
- Notes

2. Purchase Entry
- Select supplier/company
- Purchase bill number
- Purchase date
- Products purchased
- Total amount
- Paid amount
- Remaining amount
- Payment status: Paid / Partial / Pending
- Upload purchase bill photo
- Notes

3. Payment Tracking
- Add payment paid to company
- Payment mode: Cash / UPI / Bank / Cheque
- Payment date
- Remaining balance auto update

4. Dashboard Cards
Add to admin dashboard:
- Total company payment pending
- Companies with pending amount
- Today payment paid
- This month purchase amount

5. Company-wise Report
- Show each company:
  total purchase
  total paid
  remaining amount
  last payment date
  pending status

6. WhatsApp Reminder/Note
- Add button to copy message:
“Namaste [Company Name], Annadata Agri & Seeds ki taraf se ₹[amount] payment [date] ko kiya gaya hai. Kripya confirm karein.”

7. Database Changes
Create tables:
- suppliers
- purchase_bills
- purchase_bill_items
- supplier_payments

Fields:
suppliers:
id, name, mobile, address, contact_person, notes, created_at

purchase_bills:
id, supplier_id, bill_number, bill_date, total_amount, paid_amount, remaining_amount, payment_status, bill_image_url, notes, created_at

purchase_bill_items:
id, purchase_bill_id, product_id, product_name, quantity, rate, total

supplier_payments:
id, supplier_id, purchase_bill_id, amount, payment_mode, payment_date, notes, created_at

8. Stock Connection
When purchase bill is saved:
- Increase product stock automatically
- Add stock movement type: purchase
- Show stock updated message

Design:
- Hindi + English language support
- Big buttons
- Simple layout
- Mobile friendly
- Easy for shop owner

Important:
Do not break existing admin panel.
Do not remove billing/customer/stock pages.
Use existing Supabase connection.
